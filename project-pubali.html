VCF on Vxrail with Aria Suite & SRM, Design & Deployment

Client

Pubali Bank PLC, Bangladesh

Problem Statement

Design and deploy a hyper-converged infrastructure using VCF on VxRail, integrate with Aria Suite for automation and operations, and implement SRM for disaster recovery.

My Role

Lead System Engineer, responsible for the complete lifecycle of the project from design to deployment and handover.

The Solution

A comprehensive solution involving the deployment of VMware Cloud Foundation (VCF) on Dell VxRail, leveraging Aria Suite for cloud management and automation, and ensuring business continuity with VMware Site Recovery Manager (SRM).

Technologies Used

VMware VCF, Dell VxRail, Aria Suite (vRealize Suite), VMware SRM

Impact & Results

The new infrastructure provides a scalable and resilient platform for the bank's applications, with streamlined operations and a robust disaster recovery solution, significantly improving RTO and RPO.
