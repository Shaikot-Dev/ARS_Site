Nutanix HCI Solution for Midland Bank PLC

Problem Statement

Midland Bank PLC required a robust, scalable, and easy-to-manage hyperconverged infrastructure solution to replace their aging IT systems and support future growth.

My Role

System Engineer, responsible for deployment, configuration, data migration, and training.

The Solution

Deployed Nutanix AHV hyperconverged infrastructure, consolidating compute, storage, and networking into a single, unified platform. Managed complex workload migration from legacy systems and implemented comprehensive data protection strategies.

Technologies Used

Nutanix AHV, Prism Element, Prism Central, Nutanix Move, Nutanix Files

Impact & Results

Reduced operational overhead by 40%, improved application performance by 25%, and provided a highly available and resilient infrastructure for critical banking operations. Successfully trained the internal IT team on Nutanix platform management.
